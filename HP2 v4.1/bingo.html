About

This is a "Bingo" board for Harry Potter races.

To win, you must complete 5 of the tasks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The seed number is used to generate the board. Changing the seed will make a new board.

You can click on the squares to turn them green and red. This may help you organize your route planning.

For HP Bingo, there are some specific rules in place:

  • Bingo Specific Instructions: We use a custom mod for Bingo runs, developed by MaxG and EmilyPloszaj, the mod can be downloaded here and includes the following changes:
    • The game starts in the Entry Hall in the post Chamber game state with all spells unlocked
    • All areas are unlocked from the beginning of the game (including the Gryffindor Challenge and Dumbledore's Office)
    • The Gold Wizard Card Challenge is unlocked at 20 Silver Cards instead of 40
    • Night Grounds is available after clearing Willow
  • Item specific explanations:
    "Complete X Challenge (with or without jumping)": The challenge is completed upon taking the normal exit of the level, taking alternate exits or warping is not allowed (you must collect the completion star in spell challenges)
    "Complete X Challenge without jumping": You only need to do this once, you are allowed to jump on subsequent visits.
    "10 Bronze Wizard Cards without getting a health increase": Quidditch Warp out of a Bronze Card Hess
    "X different Challenge Stars": You do not need to get all of them in a single trip, dying will still reset any progress
    "8 Unique Flobberworm Mucus": You can only purchase 1, and only collect 1 from the Girl's Bathroom, buying from 2 different vendors does not count.
    "Stack 5 Mushrooms in Diffindo": You must have a single stack at least 5 mushrooms high
  • If it says to have an item, you must actually keep it. For example, if it says to have "15 Potions," you must not drink them, and you must still have it in your inventory at the time you finish getting all 5 objectives.
  • For collection goals such as "1000 beans", "15 potions", "17 challenge stars", etc, you are allowed to exceed the required amount.
